Finding Bluff Raises IP vs River Bets

A hard node for most players to find bluffs - but doing some can be extremely profitable!

Considerations

  • 1. How strong is my opponent's range?
    • (Are they capped?)

  • 2. What value combinations am I representing?
    • (Is my line credible?)

  • 3. Does it make more sense for me to call instead?
    • (Do they have many better hands that will bet/fold?)

    (If raising only folds out a small number of better hands, but is beating many other hands (bluffs), calling is probably better)


  • 4. Does my opponent likely have a sizing split here?
    • (Do they have any sizing tells to interpret?)
