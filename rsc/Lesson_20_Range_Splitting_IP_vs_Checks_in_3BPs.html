Lesson 20: Range Splitting IP vs Checks (in 3BPs)

  1. Vs 3-bet nodes are low EV - but once an opponent checks the flop, your EV is much higher, in both theory and in practice
  2. A good way to capitalize vs checks IP in 3BPs is to play a smaller bet strategy than solver would suggest

Why Small Flop Stabs Work

  1. Most opponents lack correct x/R frequencies vs small bets - for both value combos and bluffs
  2. Most opponents check/call a too-weak and face up range that folds too-often to barrels on later streets
  3. Most opponents won't find the check/call slowplays often enough

You want to bet hands that are either happy to bet/call or don't care if they have to bet/fold

As always, start with a baseline strategy (constructed via theory, not psychology!)

  • ♠ Identify the bottom of your value range vs check
  • ♠ Add an appropriate amount of premium bluffs

Good Bluffing Candidates

  1. Can make many better hands fold
  2. Can pick up equity driven draws or hands that can keep barreling
  3. Can make a value-bettable hand by the river
