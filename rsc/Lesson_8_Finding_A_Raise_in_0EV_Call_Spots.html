Finding a Raise in -EV or 0EV Call Spots

Some Rules

  1. You need to clearly see many value combos that your opponent will bet/fold
  2. You want to see as many bluffs in their range as possible
  3. Your line should be credible as a value line
  4. Always "inverser the spot" mentally to explicitly check how much credibility/fold equity the line would have on you
