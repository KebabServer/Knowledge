Lesson Thirteen: The Art of Hero Calling

Some Rules

  • Hero calling vs too-strong ranges is the single-fastest way to absolutely DESTROY your win-rate.
  • Hero calling vs too-weak ranges absolutely PRINTS in spots where your opponents don't realize how easy it is for them to have too many bluffs.
  • Good spots to hero call are when you can logically deduce the bluff region and value region and see an asymmetry in the bluff region.
  • Look for spots where there is a very clear value range and clear bluff combos with no showdown value.
  • If you have to make unrealistic assumptions about their range, then it's probably not a good spot.
  • Pot odds are of course also important.
