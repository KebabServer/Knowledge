Expanding Your Bluff Check-Raises (vs players who C-bet or fold too much)

More bluff check-raising a great exploit vs players who c-bet the flop with too weak of a range - even better if they overfold on later streets.

Things to Look For

Juice spots! Are they c-betting too much? Range betting boards they shouldn't?

Hand Qualities

  1. Make many better hands fold
  2. Interacts with the board such that you'll be able to barrel equity-driven on a lot of turns
  3. Has the potential to turn or river a value-bettable hand
  4. Blocks villain's calling range (on the flop or on future streets)
  5. \"Difficult\" bluffs - the harder it is to find bluff in a spot, the higher-EV your bluffs will be
  6. \"Good\" spots - you will get called, but you can increase your chances by choosing your spots (and player profiles) well
