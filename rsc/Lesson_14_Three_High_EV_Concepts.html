Three High EV Concepts

  • 1. Shoving vs unbalanced sizings with strong draws
    If your opponent has overbets in his turn game but is not currently using one (especially on a wet, connected board), this could indicate a weak range.
  • 2. Finding your nut bluffs in every spot
    • Bluffs that are blocking his strongest hands
    • Bluffs that have the most equity when called
  • 3. When you have enough equity to stack off, ALWAYS think about how to allow your opponent to put in more $ with the weakest parts of their range
