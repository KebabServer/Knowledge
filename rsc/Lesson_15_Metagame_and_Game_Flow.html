Lesson 15: Metagame and Game Flow

  • When you represent a very thin value range to the same player multiple times in succession, your fold equity goes down each time.
  • Keep tabs on who has seen you do what!
  • When your opponent tries to hero call you in a specific line and is wrong, your fold equity goes up in the immediate future.
  • You can preemptively widen your value range vs a specific player when the metagame and game history call for it.
  • The more crazy or non-standard the hand is, the more likely your opponents will take note and adjust.
  • Always rank your hands according to equity on each street.
  • Always use ranges and range analysis first before psychology in poker - but don't ignore it either!
