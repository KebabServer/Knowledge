Vs Aggressive Polarization

  • FOLD all of your bluffcatchers vs weak/tight regs - only find calls against aggressive regs and players who are capable of bluffing!
  • Against, players who x/R with proper aggression sometimes you should x-back strong hands IP that don't want to see a x/r and barrel.
  • First consider what they are representing - then try to call with hands that block their value

Most players will NOT be balanced when making large river nuts-or-nothing overbets.
