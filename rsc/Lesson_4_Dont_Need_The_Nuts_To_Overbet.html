You Don't Need the Nuts to Overbet (You Just Need the Best Hand)

A common misconception is that you can only overbet the strongest parts of your range for value - but really all you need is enough vs your opponent's range

Some Rules

  1. The more unbalanced the opponent, the thinner we can overbet
  2. The less you want to bluff a certain player, the thinner you want to value bet them
  3. This strategy really crushes fish and \"non-believer\" (stick!) regs
  4. The fewer value combos your opponent has that beat your hand, the larger and wider you can go for value
  5. The better you are at breaking down your opponent's actual range, the more of these spots you can find correctly
  6. Employing these plays vs other regs can really hurt their confidence and induce them to make more mistakes vs you in the future
