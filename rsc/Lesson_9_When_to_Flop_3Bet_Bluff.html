When to Flop 3-Bet Bluff to Increase Your Hand's EV

Some Rules

  1. Can be done both in and out of position
  2. You typically want to use a hand class that has low EV in alternative lines
  3. The wider and weaker your opponent's range is, the more these 3-bets punish them
  4. The less made hands or combo draws that your opponent can have to reraise, the better the spot
  5. Often times very small sizes can be used
  6. If your opponent is very weak/tight and isn't bluffing enough then just bet/fold
  7. Your blockers to their continue range will always be your first choice candidates
